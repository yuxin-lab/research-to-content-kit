# Prompt Templates

This document provides prompt templates for AI-assisted science communication workflows.

These prompts are designed to help users transform research materials, expert interviews, and medical education content into structured public-facing content.

## Important note

These prompts are for education, documentation, and science communication workflow purposes only.

They do not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, regulatory advice, or legal advice.

Users should review all AI-generated content carefully before publication.

7. Prompt usage checklist

Before using these prompts, clarify:

What is the source material?
Who is the target audience?
Is this product-related or education-only?
What platform will publish the content?
Are there medical, regulatory, or product-claim boundaries?
Does the output need Chinese, English, or bilingual content?
8. Review reminder

AI-generated output should always be reviewed by humans before publication.

For medical, health, skincare, nutrition, biotechnology, or product-related content, users should check:

Evidence level
Wording boundaries
Platform rules
Product category requirements
Expert role description
Whether claims are overstated
