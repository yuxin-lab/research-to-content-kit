## 7. Compliance-aware wording checklist

Avoid wording that may sound like diagnosis, treatment, guaranteed effect, or medical claim unless it is properly supported and legally allowed.

### Risky wording

Examples:

- Cure
- Treatment effect
- Guaranteed improvement
- Works for everyone
- Replaces medical treatment
- Clinically proven without evidence
- Drug-like mechanism claims in product promotion

### Safer alternatives

Examples:

- May help explain
- Is associated with
- May be related to
- Public education only
- Does not replace professional medical advice
- Individual results may vary
- Further research or professional evaluation may be needed
